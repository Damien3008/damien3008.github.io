      EuroMoonMars

      June 2021 - September 2021

      Commander // Analog Astronaut

      • Commander during the EuroMoonMars Poland 7 (EMMPOL7) mission organized by EuroMoonMars (EMM), the International Lunar Exploration Working Group (ILEWG), and the Analog Astronaut Training Center (AATC). This mission aimed to simulate the living conditions of a future lunar base. During a week in total isolation, I conducted 4 experiments:
        • Experiment n° 1: Growing plants (watercress) in different types of soil: Martian regolith-like soil and lunar regolith-like soil. The goal was to compare plant growth on Martian and lunar soil to that on terrestrial soil.
        • Experiment n° 2: Growing plants subjected to lunar gravity. This experiment aimed to compare the growth of watercress seeds under lunar and terrestrial gravity.
        • Experiment n° 3: Designing the payload of a lunar launcher that can be 3D-printed to send biological samples to an orbiting lunar module in emergencies. Along with another student, I designed the payload in CATIA and printed it using a 3D printer.
        • Experiment n° 4: Couple Team Foreigner (CTF). This experiment analyzed the impact of different types of relationships (romantic, friendly, none) on team dynamics during isolation. It provided insights into which relationship types might be most conducive to life in a future lunar base.
